Prof. (Dr.) Ajay Kumar

About

Dr. Ajay Kumar is a distinguished medical professional and academic, currently serving as the Director Professor in the Department of Pediatrics at Vardhman Mahavir Medical College and Safdarjung Hospital, New Delhi. As the in-charge of the Pediatric Pulmonology Division, he has pioneered significant advancements, including establishing pediatric bronchoscopy services and a dedicated pediatric pulmonology ward, both of which have greatly enhanced specialized respiratory care for children.
Dr. Kumar is also a prolific researcher who has published numerous articles in reputable national and international journals, contributing valuable insights to the fields of pediatric pulmonology and allergy. Known for his dedication to teaching, he is not only a compassionate and skilled pediatrician but also an inspiring educator, leaving a lasting impact on his students and colleagues alike.
Beyond his medical career, Dr. Kumar is a multifaceted individual who actively contributes as a social reformer, writer, singer, and blogger. His commitment to social causes, combined with his creative pursuits, reflects his passion for making a positive impact both within and outside the healthcare community.
